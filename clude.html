Home / Clude

Clude is a benefit digital club,
created for people who want to save money. In addition, it also provides an option to make money for those who want to become a reseller.

The product covers most of local health needs
and it has different discounts on supermarkets,
movie theaters and more.

About my role

I’ve started to work on the project in the early months and I leaded the teams to build a brand message, build the product and also help the board to visualize who really are the clients and design product and message for them.

Proto-Personas

The product wasn’t launched yet and every department on the company had an ideia of who the client was, because everyone was struggling to build your own part of the product.

All I have to do was to put the teams on the same room to get a sense of who are our client was.

Because the product wasn’t builded yet, we’ve decided that the proto-persona strategy could really get everyone on the same page.

The product stake holders and third-parties, had day-to-day contact with real customers, and our commercial teams had intimacy with them, so was very important to absorve insights from different areas.

I’ve invited a few team members to initiate conversations about who the clients were and leaded the proto-persona dynamic.

We’ve made a lot of meetings and rounds so we could get a sense of who our customers are.
